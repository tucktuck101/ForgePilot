## Summary

ForgePilot shall provide a human-friendly path for reading, navigating, and discovering documentation from the repository Markdown source.

## Details

The documentation must provide compact orientation through summaries and indexes, stable links to granular authoritative objects, and sufficient labels and headings for readers to understand project state and relationships. A later approved portal may add presentation and search capabilities, but is not required or implemented by this BA requirement.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Major documentation collections and singleton artefacts are reachable from compact indexes.
- Human-facing summaries identify purpose, status, and relevant next links.
- Readers can follow related detail without requiring duplicated authoritative content.
- The capability does not depend on a separately maintained documentation store.
- Portal design and implementation remain deferred until the applicable approvals are recorded.
