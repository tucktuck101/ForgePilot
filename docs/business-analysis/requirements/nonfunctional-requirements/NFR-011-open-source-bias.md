## Summary

ForgePilot shall prefer open-source tools and solutions.

## Details

The system should prioritise free, open‑source, and locally runnable tooling over paid or proprietary alternatives whenever reasonable. This includes choosing open-source frameworks, libraries, and services when selecting stacks, test runners, deployment options, and CI systems. Paid or proprietary options may be proposed only when open alternatives do not satisfy the project’s requirements, and must be accompanied by clear reasoning and an approval request.

## Notes

An open-source bias reduces cost and vendor lock-in, promoting transparency and flexibility.
