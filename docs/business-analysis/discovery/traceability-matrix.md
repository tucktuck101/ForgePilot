## Decision and Delivery Trace Rules

- Options trace to needs, evidence, risks, and GATE-001; significant later decisions must record context, alternatives, consequences, and approval under the [operating policy](approval-gates/operating-policy.md).
- [DISC-008](scope.md) is the pending authority for first-prototype inclusion. Product priority alone does not authorise implementation.
- Delivery issues use the gated issue template and must link approved scope, requirements, stories, acceptance criteria, dependencies, risks, tests, and Definition of Done.
- No delivery issue is executable while GATE-002 or GATE-003 is pending. The absence of delivery issues at this stage is intentional rather than a broken trace path.

## Coverage Rule

Every live user story and requirement must appear in this matrix through its canonical source need. New objects are not complete until the matrix and relevant indexes are updated.
