## Need

Users, maintainers, and reviewers need ForgePilot documentation to be readable, navigable, searchable, and easy to consume without changing repository Markdown as the source of truth.

## Details

The human consumption path must complement the token-efficient knowledge graph used by Codex. Summaries and indexes should orient readers, while links lead to granular authoritative objects without duplicating their content. A future generated portal may improve presentation and search, but its design and implementation require a later approved decision.

## Acceptance or Validation

The need is represented by linked options, a user story, functional and nonfunctional requirements, quality checks, and measurable success criteria. Human usability remains unvalidated until representative users complete documentation-navigation tasks.
