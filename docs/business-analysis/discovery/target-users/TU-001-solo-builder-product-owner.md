## Summary

An individual product owner who wants to turn a vague software idea into a validated prototype without manually coordinating each delivery activity.

## Goals and Context

- Reach an approved problem, brief, and prototype scope quickly.
- Delegate routine discovery and delivery work while retaining control at material gates.
- Receive a prototype and handoff that can be understood and continued.
