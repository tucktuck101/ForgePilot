## Summary

The person accountable for approving product direction, scope, risk, cost, credentials, deployment, merges, and final acceptance.

## Goals and Context

- Receive concise recommendations, alternatives, evidence, and risks.
- Record explicit approve, revise, or reject decisions.
- Prevent work from crossing an unapproved gate.
