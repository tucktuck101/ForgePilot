## Description

Provide a managed service that stores project state, orchestrates agents, exposes a UI, and controls approvals centrally.

## Analysis

- Benefits: richer coordination, observability, and cross-repository control.
- Costs: hosting, identity, security, privacy, billing, and operational complexity.
- Risks: vendor lock-in and solution scope far beyond pre-solution validation.
- Conclusion: explicitly out of prototype scope.
