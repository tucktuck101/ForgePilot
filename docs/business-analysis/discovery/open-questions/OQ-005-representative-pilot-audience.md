## Question

Who represents the first pilot audience?

## Resolution

- Answer: The first pilot audience is 3–5 solo builders or product owners who can also act as the approval authority for their pilot and who collectively represent varied levels of technical experience.
- Decision: Use this focused audience before testing separate client-approver and technical-reviewer handoffs with a mixed-role cohort.
- Decision date: 2026-06-25.
- Decision reference: Product Owner selected option A in the Codex open-question working session dated 2026-06-25.

## Consequences and Validation

- Recruitment should include meaningful variation in software-delivery experience while retaining the solo-builder/product-owner operating context.
- Each participant must be able to make or obtain the material decisions required for their pilot.
- Participant identities, consent, pilot scenarios, and evidence records remain to be established before research begins.
- The audience decision does not validate the personas or value hypotheses; those remain unvalidated until representative pilots are completed and recorded.
