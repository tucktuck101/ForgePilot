## Question

What is the current prototype-cycle baseline?

## Resolution

- Answer: No credible prototype-cycle baseline currently exists.
- Decision: Establish the initial baseline from the first three comparable ForgePilot pilot cycles rather than using retrospective estimates.
- Decision date: 2026-06-25.
- Decision reference: Product Owner selected option A in the Codex open-question working session dated 2026-06-25.

## Consequences and Validation

- No prototype-cycle efficiency claim may be made until the three comparable cycles are recorded.
- The pilot records must use a consistent cycle boundary and retain enough context to explain material differences in scope, stack, interruptions, and approval delays.
- After the third comparable cycle, update the relevant evidence, success measures, and this record with the calculated baseline.
