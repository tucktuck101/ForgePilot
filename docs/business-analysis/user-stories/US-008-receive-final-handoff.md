## Summary

## Story

As a Solo Builder, I want a final handoff package so that I can run, test, review, deploy, or continue the prototype.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Final handoff document exists.
- It includes setup, run, test, deployment, limitations, security/privacy notes, future backlog, and acceptance checklist.
