## Summary

## Story

As the Client, I want to compare the final prototype against approved scope so that I can accept, iterate, harden, deploy, or stop.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Acceptance checklist exists.
- Known limitations are documented.
- Future backlog is documented.
- Acceptance decision is recorded.
