## Summary

Capture the Codex need to size sprints according to risk, clarity, and efficiency.

## Story

As Codex, I want to choose sprint size based on risk, clarity, and token efficiency so that work remains efficient and safe.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Small sprints are used for unclear, risky, unfamiliar, approval-adjacent, or security/data/auth/deployment-heavy work.
- Larger sprints are used for clear, low-risk, repetitive, convention-following work.
- Sprint notes record the chosen sprint goal.
