## Story

As the Client, I want to approve the product brief and scope so that Codex does not build the wrong thing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Approval request explains what is being approved.
- Approval request explains why approval is needed.
- Approve/revise/reject options are clear.
- Approval status is recorded.
