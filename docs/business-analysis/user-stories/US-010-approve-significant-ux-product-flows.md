## Summary

Capture the Client need to approve significant UX or product flows.

## Story

As the Client, I want to approve material UX or product flows before heavy implementation so that expensive rework is avoided.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Codex identifies material user flows requiring approval.
- UX/product flow artefact exists where relevant.
- Risks and trade-offs are summarised.
- Codex waits for approval when the flow is material.
